# Privacy Policy for Restaurant Finder

**Last Updated:** October 3, 2024

## Overview
Restaurant Finder ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Location Data**: Zip codes you enter to search for restaurants
- **Search Preferences**: Restaurant filters including distance, price range, cuisine types, and open hours preferences
- **Visit Notes**: Personal notes and ratings you create for restaurants you've visited

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We use Google Places API which may collect basic device and usage information as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:
- Search for restaurants near your specified location using Google Places API
- Save your restaurant visit history and notes locally on your device
- Filter and display restaurant results based on your preferences
- Provide the "I'm Feeling Lucky" random restaurant selection feature

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All your visit notes, ratings, and search history are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences (mobile) or localStorage (web)
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your personal data on any remote servers
- **Third-Party Services**: Location searches are processed through Google Places API. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information about how Google handles data

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs when:
- Using Google Places API for restaurant searches (governed by Google's privacy policy)
- You choose to share restaurant information through your device's native sharing features

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data stored locally on your device
- Delete your visit history and notes at any time through the app
- Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Places API**: For restaurant search functionality
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [Google Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- All data is stored locally on your device with standard device security
- No data transmission to our servers
- API communications with Google Places are encrypted
