Welcome to Restaurant Finder Support!

We're here to help you discover amazing restaurants near you. Whether you're looking for a specific cuisine, price range, or just feeling lucky, our app makes finding your next meal easy and fun.

About Restaurant Finder

Restaurant Finder is a mobile application that helps you discover restaurants based on your preferences. Search by location, filter by cuisine type, price range, ratings, and more. Save your favorite spots and keep track of your dining adventures!

Key Features:

  • Smart Search: Find restaurants by zip code or city/state
  • Custom Filters: Distance (100 yards to 25 miles), cuisine type, price range, ratings, and "open now" option
  • My Visits: Save restaurants, add personal notes, ratings, and reviews
  • Get Directions: One-tap navigation to any restaurant using Google Maps
  • Visit Website: Quickly access restaurant websites for menus and more
  • I'm Feeling Lucky: Let the app randomly pick a restaurant for you
  • Sort Results: By distance, rating, name, or price
  • Google Reviews: Leave reviews directly from your saved visits

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I search for restaurants?
A: Enter a zip code OR city and state, select your desired filters (distance, cuisine, price, rating), and tap "Search Restaurants". Results will appear in a scrollable list.
Q: Can I search for specific restaurant names?
A: Yes! There's an optional "Restaurant Name" field at the top of the search form. Enter the restaurant name along with your location to find specific places.
Q: What cuisine types are available?
A: We support over 30 cuisine types including American, Italian, Mexican, Chinese, Japanese, Indian, Thai, Mediterranean, BBQ, Seafood, Breakfast, Kosher, and many more! Select "All Cuisine Types" or choose specific ones from the dropdown menu.
Q: How do I save a restaurant to My Visits?
A: When viewing search results, tap the "Choose This Restaurant" button on any restaurant card. It will be saved to your My Visits page where you can add notes, ratings, and reviews.
Q: How does "I'm Feeling Lucky" work?
A: After searching for restaurants, tap the orange "I'm Feeling Lucky" button at the top of the results. The app will randomly select one restaurant from your search results and display it.
Q: Can I remove restaurants I'm not interested in?
A: Yes! Each restaurant card has a checkbox in the top-right corner. Check the box to remove it from your current search results.
Q: How do I get directions to a restaurant?
A: Tap the "Get Directions" button (with map icon) on any restaurant card. This will open Google Maps with turn-by-turn directions from your current location.
Q: Can I see more than 20 results?
A: Yes! If there are more restaurants available, a "Load More" button will appear at the bottom of your results. Tap it to load the next 20 restaurants.
Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All your saved visits, notes, and ratings are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on any remote servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Q: How do I delete a saved visit?
A: Go to "My Visits" page, find the restaurant you want to remove, and tap the delete button. Your visit and all associated notes will be permanently removed.

How to Use Restaurant Finder

1. Search for Restaurants

  • Enter a zip code (e.g., "10001") OR city and state (e.g., City: "New York", State: "New York")
  • Optionally enter a restaurant name to search for specific places
  • Set your search distance (100 yards to 25 miles)
  • Select cuisine type(s) or leave as "All Cuisine Types"
  • Choose price range (tap multiple to select $, $$, $$$, $$$$)
  • Set minimum rating (0-5 stars)
  • Toggle "Open Now" if you only want currently open restaurants
  • Tap "Search Restaurants"

2. Browse and Filter Results

  • Results appear in a popup modal at the bottom of the screen
  • Tap the Sort button to sort by Distance, Rating, Name, or Price
  • Tap "I'm Feeling Lucky" for a random selection
  • Scroll through restaurant cards to see details
  • Check the box on any card to remove unwanted restaurants
  • Tap "Load More" at the bottom to see additional results

3. Take Action on Restaurants

  • Choose This Restaurant: Saves to My Visits, shows confirmation with quick actions
  • Get Directions: Opens Google Maps with navigation
  • Visit Website: Opens the restaurant's website in your browser

4. Manage Your Visits

  • Go to My Visits tab to see all saved restaurants
  • Tap a restaurant to expand and add:
    • What you ordered
    • Your personal rating (1-5 stars)
    • Your review/notes
  • Tap "Leave Google Review" to review on Google
  • Delete visits you no longer want to track

Troubleshooting

No restaurants found?

  • Try increasing the search distance
  • Broaden your filters (remove cuisine type, lower rating requirement)
  • Try a different location or zip code
  • Disable "Open Now" if enabled

App not responding?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Close and restart the app
  • Clear the app cache in your device settings
  • Make sure you're using the latest version

Wrong location results?

  • Double-check your zip code is entered correctly
  • For city/state search, ensure both fields are filled in
  • Verify the state is selected correctly from the dropdown

Directions not working?

  • Make sure Google Maps is installed on your device
  • Check that location services are enabled
  • Grant the app permission to access your location
