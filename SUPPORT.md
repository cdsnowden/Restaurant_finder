## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I search for restaurants?

Restaurant Finder offers multiple search options:
- **Restaurant Name:** Search for a specific restaurant by name
- **Zip Code:** Find restaurants near a zip code
- **City & State:** Search by city and state
- **Route Search:** Find restaurants along your travel route

### How does the Route Search work?

1. Tap "Along Route" on the search screen
2. Tap "Get Current Location" to use your GPS location
3. Enter your destination address
4. Set your maximum detour distance (1-25 miles)
5. Apply any filters (cuisine, price, rating, etc.)
6. View restaurants positioned along your route with detour information

### Can I save my favorite restaurants?

Yes! Use the "My Visits" tab to:
- Save restaurants you've visited or want to visit
- Add notes about what you ordered
- Rate your experience
- Write personal reviews
- Leave Google reviews

### How do I filter search results?

You can filter by:
- **Cuisine Type:** American, Italian, Chinese, Mexican, and more
- **Price Range:** $, $$, $$$, $$$$
- **Rating:** Minimum star rating (1-5 stars)
- **Open Now:** Only show currently open restaurants
- **Distance:** Set search radius or max detour distance

### Why aren't any restaurants showing up?

Try these solutions:
- **Broaden your filters:** Remove cuisine or price filters
- **Increase search radius:** Expand the distance range
- **Check "Open Now":** Turn off the "Open Now" filter
- **Verify location permissions:** Ensure the app has location access
- **Check your internet connection:** The app requires internet to search

### How do I update the app?

- **Android:** Open Google Play Store, search for Restaurant Finder, tap "Update"
- **iOS:** Open App Store, go to Updates, find Restaurant Finder, tap "Update"

### What permissions does the app need?

- **Location:** Required to find restaurants near you and calculate routes
- **Internet:** Required to search for restaurants and get directions

### How accurate is the route detour information?

The app calculates:
- Distance along your route to each restaurant
- Detour distance (how far off your route)
- Estimated detour time (assumes 30 mph average speed)

These are estimates based on straight-line distances and may vary from actual driving distances.

## Technical Issues

### Location Not Working
1. Check that location permissions are enabled for Restaurant Finder
2. Ensure Location Services are turned on in your device settings
3. Try restarting the app
4. Make sure you have a clear view of the sky (for GPS signal)

### App Crashes or Freezes
1. Close the app completely and reopen it
2. Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store
3. Restart your device
4. If the problem persists, email us with details

### Search Results Not Loading
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different location or search type
3. Reduce the number of filters applied
4. Close and reopen the app
