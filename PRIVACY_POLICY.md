# Privacy Policy for Restaurant Finder

**Last Updated: November 11, 2024**

## Introduction

Restaurant Finder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
- **Zip Code or City/State**: You provide this information to search for nearby restaurants
- **Device Location** (optional): Used only when you choose to get directions to a restaurant
- We do NOT track your location in the background
- We do NOT store your location data on our servers

### Locally Stored Data
The following information is stored only on your device:
- Saved restaurant visits
- Your personal notes about restaurants
- Your ratings and reviews
- Search preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:
- Search for restaurants near your specified location using Google Places API
- Display restaurant information (name, address, ratings, reviews)
- Provide directions to restaurants via Google Maps
- Save your restaurant visits and notes locally on your device

## Third-Party Services

### Google Places API
We use Google Places API to search for and display restaurant information. When you search for restaurants:
- Your search location is sent to Google Places API
- Google's Privacy Policy applies to this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We do not store or process this data ourselves

### Google Maps
When you choose to get directions:
- Your request is handled by Google Maps
- Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### URL Launcher
We use url_launcher to open:
- Restaurant websites
- Google Maps for directions
- Google Reviews pages

## Data Storage

All your personal data (saved visits, notes, ratings) is stored:
- **Locally on your device only**
- Using device storage (SharedPreferences)
- NOT on any remote servers
- NOT accessible to us or any third parties

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Collect personal information
- Store your data on servers
- Share your information with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use analytics or tracking services

The only data transmission occurs when:
- You search for restaurants (sent to Google Places API)
- You request directions (handled by Google Maps)

## Children's Privacy

Restaurant Finder does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
- Choose not to provide location information
- Control which restaurants you save to your visits

## Data Security

- All your data is stored locally on your device
- We use secure HTTPS connections for API requests
- We do not have access to your device data

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
